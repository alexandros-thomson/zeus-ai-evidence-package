## REVISED TRIGGER LOGIC

| Scenario | Action | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Track 1 granted | ΣτΕ not needed — close skeleton | — |
| Track 1 rejected (written response) | File ΣτΕ within 60 days of rejection notice date | 60 days from notice |
| **Track 1 no response by 02/06/2026** | **File ΣτΕ immediately — do NOT wait for 50-day window** | **02/06/2026 trigger** |

**Rationale:** If Track 1 receives no response by 02/06/2026, the 50-day administrative silence window will not close until ~07/07/2026 — which is 14 days after the ΣτΕ 23/06/2026 outer limit. Filing ΣτΕ requires waiting for silence to ripen OR filing preemptively on grounds of constructive rejection under Ν.2690/1999 Άρθρο 4 §1. File no later than **02/06/2026** to preserve the 23/06 ΣτΕ window with adequate prep time.
