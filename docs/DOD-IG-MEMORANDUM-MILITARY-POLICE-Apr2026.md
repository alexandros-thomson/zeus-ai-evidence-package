### A. The Victim Is a NATO Servicemember

John Kyprianos served in the **Hellenic Navy** — a branch of Greece's armed forces under NATO. ### B. NATO SOFA Applies

The **NATO Status of Forces Agreement (SOFA)**, to which both the United States and Greece are signatories, creates binding obligations for host nations regarding the legal standing of NATO servicemembers and their families. **Article VII** specifically addresses jurisdiction and the obligation of host nations to act when servicemember-related crimes are reported. Greece has been formally notified through the U.S. Embassy in Chicago (February 1, 2026) and through ΑΕΑΔ (April 3–6, 2026). No enforcement action has followed.

### C. The U.S. Has Standing to Request Greek Military Police Action
