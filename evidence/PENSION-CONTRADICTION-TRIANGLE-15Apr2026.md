## Logical Analysis

### These three positions are mutually exclusive:

**If Source 1 is true** (Κακλαμάνου: pension existed):
- Source 2 (Παπασταύρου) lied 3 times to an heir
- Source 3 (ΗΔΥΚΑ) either has incomplete records OR payments were diverted/deleted
- → Raises question: WHERE DID THE PENSION PAYMENTS GO?

**If Source 2 is true** (Παπασταύρου: never existed):
- Source 1 (Κακλαμάνου) fabricated the pension's existence
- Source 3 (ΗΔΥΚΑ) corroborates — no payments found
- → But WHY would Κακλαμάνου confirm a pension that never existed?

**If Source 3 is true** (ΗΔΥΚΑ: no records in ΕΣΕΠΣ since 2013):
- Does NOT prove pension never existed — only that ΕΣΕΠΣ has no record from 2013 onward
- Pension may have existed pre-2013 (before ΕΣΕΠΣ centralization)
- Payments may have been diverted to unauthorized recipients
- Records may have been deleted or manipulated
