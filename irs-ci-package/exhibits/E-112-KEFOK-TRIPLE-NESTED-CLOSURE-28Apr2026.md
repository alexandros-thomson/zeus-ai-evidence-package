## Headline

Three sequential myAADE eTicket cases filed against ΚΕΦΟΚ Αττικής on the same matter (AFM 044594747 access / records). Each closed without substantive answer. The closure typology evolves across layers — culminating in **fraudulent administrative act**: an assertion that a prior answer exists when none was ever produced.

This is an Article 259 ΠΚ (Breach of Duty) pattern stacked on Article 4 N.2690/1999 (silent rejection) stacked on Article 5 N.2690/1999 (motivation requirement). Layer 3 adds Article 242 ΠΚ (Ψευδής Βεβαίωση / False Certification by a Public Official) because the boilerplate "already answered" is a positive false statement of fact in an administrative document.

## Why This Matters

1. **Pattern escalation across three filings.** A single silent rejection is a routine Art. 4 breach. Three sequential closures with progressively misleading semantics is a pattern — and patterns establish intent.
2. **"Already answered" is testable and false.** No prior ΚΕΦΟΚ Αττικής written answer exists in any AADE channel — myAADE inbox, my1521 thread, ΔΕΣΥΠ protocol receipts, or postal mail. Therefore prosecutable under Art. 242 ΠΚ.
3. **Bridges ΚΕΦΟΔΕ → ΚΕΦΟΚ.** ΚΕΦΟΔΕ Αττικής (E-106 midnight phone outreach, Protocol #513) and ΚΕΦΟΚ Αττικής (this exhibit) are sister directorates within AADE Attiki. Both now documented obstructing the same AFM-044594747/051422558 inquiry through different mechanisms (phone-channel pivot vs. circular dismissal). The obstruction is **directorate-spanning** — systemic Art. 259 ΠΚ rather than directorate-specific.
