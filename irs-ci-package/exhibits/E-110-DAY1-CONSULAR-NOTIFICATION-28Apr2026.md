## Treaty Hook

**1953 US-Greece Treaty of Friendship, Commerce and Navigation:**

- **Article 26** — Information exchange between contracting parties on matters affecting their nationals.
- **Article 27** — Access to courts and administrative agencies for nationals of either party.

Greek administrative agencies (ΚΕΦΟΔΕ, ΚΕΦΟΚ, ΕΦΚΑ, ΗΔΥΚΑ, Συνήγορος, ΕΑΔ, Εισαγγελία Πειραιά, Κτηματολόγιο) have collectively denied a U.S. citizen widow access to administrative due process for 1,780 days. The treaty makes this a U.S. State Department concern, not just a Greek-domestic concern.
