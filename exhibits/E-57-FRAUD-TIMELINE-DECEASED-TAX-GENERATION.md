## 5. CRIMINAL CLASSIFICATION

1. **Tax Fraud (Art. 66 Greek Tax Code)** — Generation and collection of tax obligations for deceased person
2. **Document Fraud** — Issuance of Ekatharistiko/tax documents for non-existent taxpayer
3. **Banking Fraud** — Maintaining and operating accounts connected to deceased individual
4. **Identity Fraud** — Continued use of deceased's AFM for financial transactions
5. **Estate Fraud** — Unauthorized management of deceased's financial obligations without probate

- Multiple government systems involved (AADE, banks, utilities)
