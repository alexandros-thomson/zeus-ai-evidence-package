## 3. Legal Framework Violated

### Article 4 §2, n. 2690/1999 (KDD)
The article requires that when a petition falls outside the receiving authority competence, it must within 3 days forward the matter to the competent authority AND inform the petitioner accordingly. The phrase "enimerosi sxetika ton endiafero" requires disclosure of (a) the competent authority and (b) identifying data of the transmittal. AADE dserv disclosed NEITHER.

### Article 5, n. 2690/1999 - Right of Access
Citizen retains the right to receive a copy of the internal transmittal document; AADE silence on its existence defeats the citizen ability to track the referral chain.
