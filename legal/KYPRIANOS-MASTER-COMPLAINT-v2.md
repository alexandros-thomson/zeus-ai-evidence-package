## II. WHAT KAD 319 AND KAD 320 MEAN

On the U.S. IRS Form 1040, the taxpayer must declare their residency status:

- **CHECKBOX 319 / KAD 319:** The taxpayer claims to be a RESIDENT OF A FOREIGN COUNTRY (i.e., Greece)
- **CHECKBOX 320 / KAD 320:** The taxpayer is a RESIDENT OF THE UNITED STATES

These are mutually exclusive. You cannot be both. The selection determines the taxpayer's entire tax treatment, including eligibility for Foreign Tax Credits under IRS Form 1116 and compliance with the U.S.-Greece Double Taxation Treaty (Article 4).
